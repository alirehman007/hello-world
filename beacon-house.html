Welcome to Beaconhouse

The Beaconhouse School System has risen from its modest
beginnings in 1975 as Les Anges Montessori Academy to
become a major force in the education world. With an ever-
expanding base, already established in the UK, Malaysia,
the Philippines, Pakistan, the UAE, Oman, Belgium and
Thailand, Beaconhouse is one of the largest private school
networks in the world
